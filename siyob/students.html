For students

“Siyob” is a diversified holding company of consumer goods production that have been serving the market of Uzbekistan for more than 10 years. The holding includes Pure Milky, Agro Oil, Ehtiyot Qism Savdo, Vkusnyatovo, Afrosiyob Jeans and others.

Group of companies Siyob invites ambitious, enthusiastic and energized students to undergo summer practice at one of the companies mentioned above.

Summer internship for students in "Group of companies Siyob" is a chance:

  • - To gain valuable professional experience in various fields of business (the production of dairy products, the production of oil, the production of tires and spare parts, the textile industry, the production of PET preforms, farming and others). You will be involved in real business projects.
  • - Learn from the mechanisms within the large company.
    • - Meet new interesting people and professionals.
  • - Developing not only professionally, but contributing to the personal growth also.

Mandatory requirement: Students of universities of the 2nd, 3rd, 4th courses.
