The process and principles of personnel selection

We believe that it is possible to take a leading position in the market only if first-class specialists with full dedication and enthusiasm, for whom work is a way of self-realization are able to work in the company. We strive to create an organization that would attract, develop and retain the best of the best. The goal of our strategy for the selection of new employees is to attract the most talented and capable specialists to our business.

When deciding to hire a new employee, we strive to select the most talented candidates. We prefer specialists from the largest FMCG companies, and those who not only meet the requirements of the position, but also have obvious growth potential in the Group of companies Siyob.

Recruitment of experienced managers is an important condition for our overall success. Our goal is to attract talented managers with extensive experience in the business field, with both excellent technical skills and leadership skills.

Other methods of replenishing the staff are a set of high-potential graduates of higher educational institutions.

The process of recruiting new employees is based on the universal candidate assessment system, which is used by all divisions of our company.

This system allows you to make the most objective decision and evaluate the managerial abilities and personal qualities of a potential employee of the company.
